Cheesy Garlic Broccoli

Description

This dish is a showcase of broccoli goodness, and if you know someone that doesn’t care for this delicious vegetable, this is the dish to cook them!

Ingredients

  • 3 crowns broccoli
  • 1 ¾ cups shredded cheddar cheese(175 g)
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Toss broccoli florets in olive oil and minced garlic.
  2. Spread on a baking sheet.
  3. Top with grated parmesan and roast at 400°F (200°C) for 15 mins.
