Baked Brie in Puff Pastry

Description

If you love gooey cheese, then this baked brie in puff pastry will leave you impressed. It’s the perfect snack food and only requires three very simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 125g (4.4 oz) Wheel of Brie Cheese
  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg

Steps

  • Wrap a wheel of brie in store-bought puff pastry.
  • Brush with beaten egg.
  • Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 20 mins until golden.
